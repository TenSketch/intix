Backpacking the Coffee Country

  Coorg, Karnataka, India
  21-Dec-2017 To 26-Jun-2018
  07:30 AM To 07:35 PM
  18500

About:

Rendezvous Karnataka's Western Ghats this Christmas, backpacking 6 days into the heart of Coffee Country.

Brief Itinerary:

  1. Day 1:  Arrival in Chikmaglur after 4 pm and retiring to the homestay for the night.
  2. Day 2:  Visit to Mullyanagiri view point, Hebbe falls, Tata Coffee museum and Hirekolale lake.
  3. Day 3:  Visit Belur-Halebidu. Post lunch, catch a local KSRTC bus to Madikeri.
  4. Day 4:  Visit to Bhagamanadala temple, Bylakuppe Monastery and Abbi Falls.
  5. Day 5:  Wild safari in Nagarhole National Park, visit to Iruppu falls followed by Mandalpatti jeep safari.
  6. Day 6:  Bid goodbye to the Western Ghats and start return journey to Bengaluru.

Highlights:

  • Plantation Stay.
  • Waterfalls trek.
  • Wild Safari.
  • Visit to Heritage Sites.
  • View of the rolling Western Ghats.
  • Cuppa coffee morning and evening. (Best thing about the trip)
